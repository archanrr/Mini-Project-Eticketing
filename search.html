ABOUT US

Who we are!

FASTBUS Online is one of the inter-city bus ticketing service in the world. fastbus is an initiative of SSN college website owned and managed by Archan Rajaram R 3rd Year student.

Founded in 2017 in Chennai, India, FASTBUS now connects various cities across the Tamilnadu. FASTBUS has revolutionized the online bus ticket booking industry by bringing together over 200 bus operators covering over 100,00 routes across TAMIL NADU.

redBus provides bus travellers, the most uncomplicated and hassle-free booking experience ever.
Choose your destination, select bus based on user ratings, view the seat layout, choose convenient seats, and book your ticket in just a few clicks!
SCOPE:

The FASTBUS experience does not end with the booking.
In a series of industry leading features, FASTBUS offers navigation to bus boarding points, real-time tracking of buses and information on rest stops.

A dedicated 24*7 customer-care team is available to cater to all the passengers needs while they are on-the- go.
